One, Less Than Ten

The Rules of the Game

  1. The player with the larger number wins unless...
  2. When total of both sign numbers added together equal 9 or 11; the player with the lower number wins.
  3. If the same sign is used by both players the result is a tie and that sign used by both players is no longer a valid option for the remainder of the game
  4. Play a number of rounds until their is a clear winner
  5. The game will also end immediately when a player wins with an 1 beating 10

Welcome

One, Less Than Ten is a game that came to me in a dream. It is a lot like Rock, Paper, Scissors but with a little math involved.

Fun Fact:On January 18th 2024, I literally did have a dream. In the dream I was talking to someone. I don't remember about what. I don't remember a lot about the dream. But near the end of the dream I said something like "I can make a quick game on the fly". And they said "no you can't!" And I proceeded to teach this person how to play this game as I made it up. When I woke up, I wrote it down.

The Rules of the Game

  1. Two players square off
  2. On the count of 3 both assume a hand sign
  3. The valid hand signs are numbers 1 through 10
  4. The player with the larger number wins

    5. EXCEPT:

  5. When total of both sign numbers added together equal 9

    6. OR

  6. The total of both sign numbers added together equals 11

    7. In my dream, i used "added together minus 10 equals 1" because the dream was really selling the name of the game.

    THEN

  7. The player with the lower number wins.
  8. If the same sign is used by both players the result is a tie and that sign used by both players is no longer a valid option for the remainder of the game
  9. Play a number of rounds until their is a clear winner

    10. OR

  10. The game will also end immediately when a player wins with an 1 beating 10

*Optional High-Stakes Gambling Rule: Each sign used by a player is no longer valid for that player for the remainder of the game. Place bets between each throw.
